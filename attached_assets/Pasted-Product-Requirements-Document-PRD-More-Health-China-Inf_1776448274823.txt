Product Requirements Document (PRD)

More Health China Influencer Dashboard

Project Type: Seven-Figure Enterprise Platform
Client: More Health
Target Users: China-based distributors rebranded as Influencers / Brand Partners
Primary Platforms: Mobile-first Web App + Desktop SaaS Dashboard
Design Standard: Tesla-level premium simplicity, modern fintech SaaS, Apple-quality UX

⸻

1. Executive Summary

Build a next-generation influencer dashboard for More Health that replaces outdated MLM-style back offices with a modern creator-commerce operating system.

This dashboard should make users feel like:

* They are building a business
* They are growing a personal brand
* They are earning real income
* They are part of a premium modern company
* Their dashboard is clear, motivating, and exciting

The system must avoid old-school MLM language. We will translate legacy functionality into modern influencer / affiliate terminology.

This is not a “back office.”

This is an:

Influencer Business Hub

⸻

2. Core Product Vision

Transform a traditional distributor account into a modern creator dashboard where users can:

* Shop products
* Enroll customers
* Grow referrals
* Track earnings
* Manage subscriptions
* Monitor business growth
* Access their personal storefront
* Withdraw earnings
* Manage profile & account settings
